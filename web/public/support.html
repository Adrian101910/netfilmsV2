Frequently Asked Questions

How can I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our support team by filling out the form below or sending an email to support@netfilms.com.

How can I update my payment information?

To update your payment information, go to the "Settings" section in your account and click on "Payment Details".
